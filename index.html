Alma Palomares-Juarez is a cosmetologist with over 7 years of experience. Having gained her licensed out of Porter High School in Porter, Texas, she started her career by mastering men's hair cuts. While taking a brief break from the industry, Alma was able to maintain her expertise by doing freelance make-up, hair cut and color, as well as men's cuts. Upon moving to Seattle, Washington, Alma soon began as a colorist apprentice at Gene Juarez Salon and Spa studying under several master colorists before becoming a master colorist herself.
